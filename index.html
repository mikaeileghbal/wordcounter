      Word Counter

      The Word Counter is a dynamic online tool used for counting words, characters, spaces and sentences. in real time, along with statistics of most and least used words. Writers leverage it to improve word choice and writing style.

      How to use

      Use The Word Counter by

      1. Typing directly into the text area above
      2. Pasting in your content
      3. Browse for files on your device
      4. Drag & Drop your file to the drop area

      Word and character counts will display at the top, and your most frequently used keywords will appear to the right.

      How it works

      All word counting is made on the go, there is no need to press any "submit" buttons to make the calculation and therefore no time is lost for this procedure. You can type the text and instantly see how many words you got so far. Also, we do not store any information that is typed or pasted in the word counting box.
